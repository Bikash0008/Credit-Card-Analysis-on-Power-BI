
Credit Card Analysis on PowerBI

📌 Problem Statement

Lack of visibility into customer spending behavior and revenue drivers

Difficulty in identifying high-value customers and profitable segments

No clear understanding of card usage patterns (Swipe, Chip, Online)

Limited insights into spending categories and transaction trends

Inability to make data-driven decisions for revenue optimization

🔍 Key Insights

💰 Total revenue reached ~57M with 45.5M transactions analyzed

👤 High-income group contributes the largest share of revenue (~40%)

💼 Businessman & White-collar segments are top revenue generators

🎯 Age group 40–60 shows highest spending behavior

💳 Blue card category dominates with ~80% of total revenue

🛍️ Top spending categories: Bills, Entertainment, and Fuel

📱 Swipe transactions are most preferred over Chip and Online

🌍 Top-performing states: CA, TX, NY

💑 Married customers contribute slightly higher revenue than others

📊 Analytical Findings

Identified customer segmentation based on income, job type, and age group

Discovered revenue concentration in specific card categories (Blue cards)

Analyzed quarterly trends, showing stable revenue (~14M per quarter)

Evaluated transaction behavior patterns across different usage types

Measured customer satisfaction score (avg ~3.19) to assess experience

Correlated income levels with spending patterns to identify high-value customers

Highlighted category-wise spending trends for targeted marketing strategies

🚀 Business Impact

Enables businesses to target high-value customers more effectively

Helps in designing personalized offers and loyalty programs

Supports card upgrade strategies (Blue → Gold/Platinum)

Improves decision-making using data-driven insights

Potential to increase revenue efficiency by 15–20%
